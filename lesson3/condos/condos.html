The Monolithcondominium, designed by Latiska Marovska, is 50-story architectural marvel in one of Brooklyn's most chic and sought-after locations. Floor-to-ceiling windows offer sweeping, stunning views of Manhattan.

Amenities include roof deck, spa, swimming pool, racquetball court, and private screening room. The concierge/doorman team is available 24 hours a day.

Here, at last, is a condominium that successfully combines the highest standards of design with convenience, comfort, and value. Near to art galleries, restaurants, public transport, and the business districts of Brooklyn and Manhattan.

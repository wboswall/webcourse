Welcome

Books n' Beans is an artisanal coffee and book shop located in the heart of downtown Northampton. We pride ourselves on serving the highest quality organic coffee combined with rare, hard-to-find vintage books.

The following is some feedback from friendly people that stops in and enjoys the warm atmosphere and picks up their favorite brew and something to eat.

"The coffee and tea are always freshly brewed and the food is always fresh and delicious" ~ Susan

"The staff is really nice and courteous and will help you in any way that they can" ~ Randy
